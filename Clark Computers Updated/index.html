Introduction.

My name is Carson Clark, I'm 22 years old and I've always been passionate about technology. After working as a Sales Consultant at Best Buy and a Computer Technician in Geek Squad, I realized that there is a desperate need for affordable quality computer services in Victoria. That's why I created Clark Computers. I enjoy working with both people and computers, and I look forward to getting in touch with you.

If I can't fix your computer issue, there's no charge. Obviously.

What I can do.

I'm the guy to call when you need help removing a virus, installing new hardware, help transferring data from a multitude of devices, speeding up that slow computer, or really anything else. I love working with Macs, PC's, Tablets, and just about any other device you can imagine.
